Music

Command Description Usage
.play If no parameters are specified, acts as .next 1 command. If you specify a song number, it will jump to that song. If you specify a search query, acts as a .q command .play .play 5 .play Tiny Little Adiantum
.queue Queue a song using keywords or a link. You must be in a voice channel. .q Tiny Little Adiantum
.queuenext Works the same as .queue command, except it enqueues the new song after the current one. You must be in a voice channel. .qn Tiny Little Adiantum
.queuesearch Search for top 5 youtube song result using keywords, and type the index of the song to play that song. You must be in a voice channel. .qs Tiny Little Adiantum
.listqueue Lists 10 currently queued songs per page. .lq .lq 2
.next Goes to the next song in the queue. You have to be in the same voice channel as the bot. You can skip multiple songs, but in that case songs will not be requeued if .rcs or .rpl is enabled. .n .n 4
.stop Stops the music and preserves the current song index. Stays in the channel. .s
.destroy Completely stops the music and unbinds the bot from the channel. .d
.pause Pauses or Unpauses the song. .p
.volume Sets the music playback volume (0-100%) .vol 25
.songremove Remove a song by its # in the queue, or 'all' to remove all songs from the queue and reset the song index. .srm 4
.soundcloudqueue Queue a soundcloud song using keywords. You must be in a voice channel. .sq Tiny Little Adiantum
.soundcloudpl Queue a Soundcloud playlist using a link. .scpl <soundcloud_playlist_link>
.nowplaying Shows the song that the bot is currently playing. .np
.shuffle Shuffles the current playlist. .plsh
.playlist Queues up to 500 songs from a youtube playlist specified by a link, or keywords. .pl <youtube_playlist_link>
.radio Queues a radio stream from a link. Must be a direct mp3 radio stream such as .m3u, .pls, .asx, or .xspf .ra <radio_link>
.move Moves the bot to your voice channel. .mv
.movesong Moves a song from one position to another. .ms 4>2
.reptcursong Toggles repeat of current song. .rcs
.rpeatplaylst Toggles repeat of all songs in the queue. .rpl
.autoplay Toggles autoplay - When the song is finished, automatically queue a related Youtube song. .ap
